1. Petite Terre: A Nature Reserve with Sharks, Rays, and Turtles

A preserved paradise accessible only by boat! The Petite Terre archipelago is a protected natural jewel, perfect for wildlife and snorkeling lovers. Here, you can observe:

  • Lemon sharks, impressive but harmless.
  • Stingrays, gliding gracefully underwater.
  • Sea turtles, swimming in crystal-clear waters.

The island is uninhabited and offers a postcard-worthy landscape with white sand beaches and coconut trees.
